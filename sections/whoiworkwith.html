Who i work with

Frank

As co-founder of Frank, I shaped one of the UK’s most respected PR agencies. Frank has represented some of the nation’s best-known brands – from Coca-Cola to Weetabix – and is one of the industry’s most decorated agencies.

If you’ve ever squeaked ‘Simples’ when seeing a meerkat then you’ve experienced the power of a Frank campaign. In 2007, I helped facilitate the sale of Frank to Australian marketing services firm Enero (ASX:EGG). A subsequent deal saw a proportion of equity return to the management team. In 2020, I stepped down from my day-to-day role as managing partner of Frank to become a non-executive director. In early 2021, the Frank management team completed an MBO, returning Frank to a fully-owned independent business.

Lord Sugar

I have acted as official spokesperson and PR adviser to Lord Sugar for over 20 years, and have handled the PR for all The Apprentice winners and their companies since the show started in 2005.

I don’t intend to hear ‘You’re fired!’ directed at me any time soon.

AAR Group

I have partnered with AAR, and am responsible for leading and expanding their consultancy practice. I sat on the other side of the table for many years so I’ve been able to put my expertise to good use and provide them with valuable insight.
